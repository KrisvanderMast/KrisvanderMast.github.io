If you like Windows 8, coding or UX and want to either show off what you created or learn new tricks then be sure to come to the [Microsoft Windows 8 Summer App-a-thons](/images/metromap.html) in the coming months. There’s going to be one in Blankenberge and one in Antwerp (/images/where I’m also going to). The concept is easy: come over, bring your laptop and friends and have fun while being creative. Oh yeah, there will also be prizes.
