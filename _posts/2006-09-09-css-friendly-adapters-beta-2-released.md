Just found this on the blog of [Brian Goldfarb](/images/746510.aspx). [Get the goods here](/images/cssadapters).

A small overview of what's new in the Beta 2 release:

* More adapters
 + [GridView](/images/GridView.aspx)+ [Login](/images/Login.aspx)+ [ChangePassword](/images/ChangePassword.aspx)+ [PasswordRecovery](/images/PasswordRecovery.aspx)+ [CreateUserWizard](/images/CreateUserWizard.aspx)+ [LoginStatus](/images/LoginStatus.aspx)* Menu adapter now supports:
 + [Postback.](/images/WalkThrough.aspx#SimpleMenu)+ Static and dynamic item templates.+ [Distinct classes to mark the selected menu item, its parents and children](/images/WalkThrough.aspx#SimpleMenu)+ [Automatic masking of underlying peek-a-boo content (/images/like tags)](/images/Menu.aspx)* TreeView adapter now supports
 + [Checkboxes.](/images/WalkThrough.aspx#CheckboxesTreeView)+ [Populate-on-demand](/images/WalkThrough.aspx#PopulateOnDemandTreeView)+ [Postback (/images/including emulation of the SelectedNodeChanged, TreeNodeCheckChanged and TreeNodePopulate events)](/images/WalkThrough.aspx#SimpleTreeView)+ [Automatic re-expansion to the selected node upon postback](/images/WalkThrough.aspx#SimpleTreeView)+ [Distinct classes to mark the selected menu item, its parents and children](/images/WalkThrough.aspx#SimpleTreeView)* Dozens and dozens of fixes and enhancements requested via the [forum](/images/ShowForum.aspx).
